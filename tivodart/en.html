An artist,Tivodar Artur, was born in December 1963 in Transcarpathia. Now he lives in Uzhgorod, Transcarpathia, Ukraine. As an artist Arthur has formed in the studio of I. M. Shutev, the People's Artist of Ukraine. He works in the fields of landscape-painting, icon-painting, and decorative design. In his works the artist uses different matirials: leather, ornamental stones. Tivodar Artur participated in different exhibition including international.
